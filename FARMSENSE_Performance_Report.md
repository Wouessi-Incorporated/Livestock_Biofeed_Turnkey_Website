## Return on Investment Calculator

### ROI Formula Factors

**Annual Benefits (B):**
- Mortality reduction savings: (Previous mortality rate - New mortality rate) × Animal value × Number of animals
- Feed efficiency gains: Feed cost reduction × Number of animals
- Veterinary cost savings: (Previous vet costs - New vet costs) × Number of animals
- Labor cost savings: Labor time reduction × Hourly rate × 365 days
- Revenue improvements: Quality premiums × Production volume

**Annual Costs (C):**
- Software licensing fees
- Maintenance and support costs
- Connectivity and data costs
- Staff training and operational costs

**ROI Calculation:**
ROI = (B - C) / Initial Investment × 100
